Complaints Log

Todo

Placeholder - copied JP’s flow chart. In the future this page should be a breakdown of the complaints log record and the below should be expanded into a tutorial.

On receipt of a complaint regarding a premises to which the Fire Safety Order applies create a complaint log on the CFRMIS premises file, with an SP28 job.

Take action appropriate to the complaint, this , where possible, be within 24 hours. This may be a phone call or an immediate visit to the premises, etc.

Once initial action is taken the complaint log must be updated and the Action Date filled in.

The SP28 job is to be kept open with all time recorded against this job until the complaint is finally closed down, the complaint log must be updated with all relevant information, the action date must stay as the date of first action. If whilst you are dealing with the SP28 you decide to do an RI or FI etc in addition to dealing with the complaint, then the SP28 is to be closed down once the complaint has been dealt with and the new RI or FI created.
