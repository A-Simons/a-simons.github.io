Data Screen

This region of the CFRMIS interface displays your open tabs. This will always include the Welcome Screen, and this will be the tab that you see when you launch CFRMIS.

CFRMIS has thousands of different tables of information, to make this information manageable we open tabs to access specific types of information. Each tab may in turn be divided into smaller panes containing specific types of information.

Form Viewer

Forms allow you to query or read information relating to specific records in the database. In the example shown above we see a ../../tables/premises form. We will look more at specific forms throughout this guide.

Table Viewer

Tables present information relating to multiple records in the database. In the example shown above we see a table of results - a list of premises records which match the query made in the premises form. As with forms, we will look at specific tables later in this guide.

Specific Viewers

You will also find a number of specific viewers in CFRMIS which don’t reappear as commonly as the form and table viewers, which will be explained in their respective sections.
