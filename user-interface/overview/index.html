Overview

Once you have navigated to CFRMIS you should be greeted by a screen that looks similar to the screenshot below. What you see might look a little different, because CFRMIS will be resized to fit the device you are using. Depending on your user profile, some of the menus you see below might not be visible.

CFRMIS’ interface is divided into a number of regions:

  • The Taskbar

  • The Status Bar

  • The Record Explorer

  • Tabs

  • The Main Screen

CFRMIS has been developed in response to the needs of dozens of fire and rescue services across the country and can record a great deal of information, a lot of which we as a service do not use. This guide is focused on making sure that we get the most out of CFRMIS and that it can be an effective tool to help us keep the people of Northamptonshire safer.
