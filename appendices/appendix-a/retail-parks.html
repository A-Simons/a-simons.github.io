Retail, Industrial and Business Parks

When creating or updating a premises within a retail, industrial or business park on CFRMIS the General style guidelines should be adhered to, other than as below.

Property Number

The property or unit number within the park, if available. If this is not available refer to however the business numbers the property in the address given on its own website (not the google card which is presented when searching for the business). If no number is given in the address then make no entry in this field.

Property Name

This field is for indicating substructures/units within larger parks, for example in Riverside Retail Park is a Subway and a Costa. These don’t have property numbers but are both found within the “Pod Unit” in the carpark.

Address Line 1

This is the name of the retail, industrial or business park

  • This should be styled consistently with however the park is presented to the public. If there is a sign, typical with retail parks, then this is likely to show the name of the park.

  • This field is to contain the word “retail”, “industrial” or “business” as appropriate in order to make sure it is possible to retrieve information relating to these sites in the future.

  • This field should be identical in all premises found within any given retail, industrial or business park.

Address Line 2

The street address for the premises.

Easting & Northing

Premises within retail, industrial and business parks are more prone to error when using the gazetteer to identify their coordinates. It is important to use the Using Edit Map process to ensure that we have accurate coordinates for each premises.
