Query Other Building

One of the most commonly carried out tasks in CFRMIS is finding the right Premises Record. As most of the work you do will be associated with a premises record it is incredibly important to make sure that you’re working with the right one.

This isn’t always simple or straightforward, though. As was discussed earlier, we haven’t always been very consistent or careful about the information that we put into CFRMIS and as a result it can sometimes be more difficult to find the right record than you might expect.

Search

You can make a query using any of the information which is recorded as part of a Premises Record. Every empty field that you see in the Query Other Building Form can be used as part of your query. If you put some information into a field CFRMIS will return every record which matches what you put into that field.

Note

The information that you put into a field is a term - a Premises Record will only appear in the results of a query if the information that it has in the respective field matches the term that you have entered.

This also works with no terms at all.

Press Search. A results pane will open at the bottom of the screen displaying every single Premises Record which is not a domestic premises.
